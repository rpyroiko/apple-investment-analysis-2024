# Apple Inc. (AAPL) Investment Analysis

## 1. Company Background (Wikipedia)
Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company specializing in consumer electronics, software, and online services. It is one of the Big Five American IT companies alongside Alphabet, Amazon, Meta Platforms, and Microsoft. Founded on April 1, 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, Apple has grown to become the largest technology company by revenue, with $383.285 billion in 2023 and a market cap exceeding $3.5 trillion. The company’s primary revenue sources are iPhone sales, Services (App Store, iCloud, Apple Music, etc.), and Mac sales. Apple employs over 160 k people and is headquartered in Cupertino, California.
